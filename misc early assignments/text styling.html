Zeal Required In Love.

BY ROBERT HERRICK


I'll do my best to win whene'er I woo:

That man loves not who is not zealous too.



Robert Herrick, "Zeal Required In Love.". Public Domain Poetry, Mick, 2005, https://www.public-domain-poetry.com/robert-herrick/zeal-required-in-love-18658. Accessed 10/1/2022.
